Velocity Commands


  • changechannel
    p!changechannel
         Change the Bot's Voice Channel to the Channel you are in.
         Aliases: cc

  • connect
    p!connect
         Connects the Bot to the Voice Channel you are currently in.
         Aliases: c

  • disconnect
    p!disconnect
         Disconnects the Bot from the Voice Channel & Stops Audio.
         Aliases: dc

  • invite
    p!invite
         Gives the link for inviting this bot to your Server.
         Aliases: addbot

  • reset
    p!reset
         Reset the Bot Settings of the Server.
         Aliases: rs, restart

  • settings
    p!settings {SETTING_NAME} {VALUE}
         View and Edit Bot Settings in the Server.
         Aliases: set, setting

  • stats
    p!stats
         Provides some Statistics about the Bot.
         Aliases: ping

  • play
    p!play
         Play from a Supported Link or Search for Music with the Provided Text.
         Aliases: p, pl, yt

  • loop
    p!loop
         Loops the Currently Playing Song. Use the command again to Disable the Loop.
         Aliases: l

  • shuffle
    p!shuffle
         Randomly Play a Song in the Current Queue.
         Aliases: sh

  • pause
    p!pause
         Pauses the AudioPlayer.
         Aliases:

  • queue
    p!queue
         Shows the Number of Songs in Queue.
         Aliases: q, playlist

  • stop
    p!stop
         Stops the AudioPlayer and clears the Song Queue.
         Aliases: st

  • move
    p!move
         Moves a track in the queue.
         Aliases: mv

  • skip
    p!skip
         Skips the Currently Playing Song.
         Aliases: s

  • clear
    p!clear
         Clears the Queue.
         Aliases: cl

  • previous
    p!previous
         Plays the Previous Song again.
         Aliases: prev, back

  • resume
    p!resume
         Resumes the AudioPlayer.
         Aliases:

  • songinfo
    p!songinfo
         Shows details about the Song Currently being Played.
         Aliases: si

  • history
    p!history
         Shows the last played songs.
         Aliases:

  • volume
    p!volume
         Changes the Volume of the AudioPlayer.
         Aliases: vol
